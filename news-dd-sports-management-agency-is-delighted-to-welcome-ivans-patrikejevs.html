D.D Sports Management Agency is delighted to welcome Ivans Patrikejevs!

We are proud to announce that talented Latvian midfielder Ivans Patrikejevs has officially joined D.D Sports Management Agency! The 20-year-old joins our growing roster as we continue to discover and represent exceptional talent from across Europe and beyond.

Who is Ivans Patrikejevs?

Full Name Ivans Patrikejevs
Age 20 years old
Nationality Latvian
Position Midfielder
Height 1.87m

Standing at an impressive 1.87m, Ivans is a commanding presence in midfield. At just 20 years old, he already carries the physical attributes and technical foundation of a player built for the highest levels of the game. His combination of size, composure, and midfield intelligence make him a genuinely exciting prospect.

Latvian football continues to produce technically astute players who are making their mark across European competitions, and Ivans is very much part of that next generation. We are excited to be part of his journey and to help open doors to the opportunities his talent deserves.

The Road Ahead

Joining D.D Sports Management Agency marks an important milestone in Ivans' professional career. Our team is committed to providing him with world-class representation, a personalised career plan, and the platform to showcase his ability to clubs across Europe.

Our Plans for Ivans

  • Securing consistent first-team opportunities at a competitive professional level
  • Strategic exposure to European clubs and international scouting networks
  • Full support in technical development, personal branding, and professional growth
  • A clear long-term pathway toward top-tier club football and international recognition

Ivans brings ambition, physicality, and a hunger to succeed — exactly the qualities we look for in every player we represent. We're fully invested in helping him reach the top.

A warm welcome to Ivans and his family — we're proud to have you on board and excited for what we'll build together!
