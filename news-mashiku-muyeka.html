D.D Sports Management Agency Welcomes Mashiku Hans Muyeka!

We are thrilled to announce the signing of talented Namibian striker Mashiku Hans Muyeka — also known as "Morata" — to the D.D Sports Management Agency family!

Hailing from Kaenda Village in Namibia's Zambezi Region, Mashiku's journey is a true inspiration — from playing on dusty village fields and rising through the ranks in the lower divisions, to making a massive impact in his debut season in the Namibia Premier Football League with Mighty Gunners FC.

Player Profile

Known for his clinical finishing, pace, and goal-poaching instincts, Mashiku quickly established himself as one of the most exciting young talents in Namibian football.

Welcome to the team, Mashiku! We look forward to an exciting journey together.
