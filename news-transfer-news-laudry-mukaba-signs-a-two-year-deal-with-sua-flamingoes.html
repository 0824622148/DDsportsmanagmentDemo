Transfer News: Laudry Mukaba Signs a Two-Year Deal with Sua Flamingoes

We are pleased to announce that Laudry Mukaba has signed a two-year contract with Sua Flamingoes, with an option for an additional year.

We extend our best wishes to Laudry Mukaba for a successful season as he prepares to compete in the Botswana Premier Division. We anticipate that his exceptional goal-scoring abilities will be prominently displayed on this platform.

Laudry Mukaba's agent, Dontè Dorlly, provided the following statement:

"Discussions are currently underway with several prominent clubs interested in monitoring his performance this season. I have engaged in numerous conversations with clubs in Egypt, Algeria, and Morocco since the opening of the transfer window, highlighting his prolific goal-scoring record. While many clubs have expressed interest, the current lack of professional game experience has temporarily precluded his immediate integration into their squads.

Furthermore, his limited national team experience has impacted his progression. However, we anticipate that many teams will closely observe his performance this season, and should all go favorably, we foresee his transition to a more significant opportunity."
