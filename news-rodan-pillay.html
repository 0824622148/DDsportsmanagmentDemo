Warm Welcome to Rodan Pillay — Our New Head of Scouting!

We are thrilled to announce that Rodan Pillay has officially joined the D.D Sports Management Agency team as our Head of Scouting!

Name Rodan Pillay
Role Head of Scouting
Expertise Football Analyst & Scout
Club Experience Barrow AFC
Education Florida College
Nationality South African (Gauteng)

Rodan brings a wealth of knowledge and passion for football to our agency. As a dedicated Football Analyst and Scout with experience at clubs including Barrow AFC, he has built a strong reputation for identifying talent, conducting in-depth opposition analysis, and supporting player development pathways. His sharp eye for detail and deep understanding of the game make him a valuable addition to our growing team.

A proud South African with roots in the Gauteng region, Rodan has previously been associated with Florida College and continues to contribute to the local and international football scene. His expertise in talent identification and recruitment will play a key role in helping us discover and nurture the next generation of stars.

Please join us in giving Rodan a massive welcome! We're excited about the energy, insight, and dedication he brings to D.D Sports Management Agency.

Want to learn more about Rodan and our full leadership team? Visit our About page.
