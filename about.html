Our Story

About D.D. Sports Management Agency

Established in 2017 by President Dontè Barry Dorlly, D.D. Sports Management Agency was founded with one clear mission: to be South Africa's premier pathway to European football.

While over 50 agencies operate across South Africa, too many talented players remain trapped in a local rat race with no real route abroad. We exist to change that.

D.D. Sports Management is built as a professional player export and development powerhouse — connecting elite South African talent directly with European clubs, academies, and opportunities. We don't just represent players. We sell them.

We have created a structured, proven system that prepares players mentally, tactically, and commercially for the demands of European football. Our focus is simple: identify high-potential talent, develop them to European standards, and secure lucrative moves that benefit the player, their family, their club, and South African football as a whole.

West Africa continues to produce wave after wave of players heading to Europe's top leagues because their ecosystem is built around player export — from grassroots to senior national teams. South Africa has the raw talent. What we've lacked is the right infrastructure, the right connections, and the right ambition.

That changes now.

At D.D. Sports Management, we treat football as the multi-billion-dollar global business it is. We reject short-term comfort and self-serving agendas. Instead, we deliver long-term career planning, elite exposure, and direct pathways to clubs in Europe.

We are not another local agency hoping for the best.
We are a selling machine — strategically positioning South African players to break into European football, develop at the highest level, and help our nation compete on the international stage once again.

Our goal is ambitious but clear: Produce the next generation of South African stars who don't just reach Europe — they succeed there. Players who return stronger, wiser, and ready to inspire the next wave.

If you're a serious player ready to move beyond local comfort and chase a professional career in Europe, D.D. Sports Management Agency is your direct path.

The Agent Behind the Agency

Meet the Founder

Dontè Barry Dorlly

President & Founder — D.D. Sports Management Agency

FIFA Licensed
8+ Years Experience
3,000+ European Contacts
50+ Deals Closed

Born in Pietermaritzburg, KwaZulu-Natal, and raised in the vibrant streets of Johannesburg, my football journey began on the dusty pitches where dreams are forged every day.

I pursued my passion as a player until 2017, when an injury sidelined me. Instead of stepping away, I chose to stay deeply involved — analysing matches with my teammates, diving into coaching books, and studying the game from a tactical and managerial perspective. That injury became the turning point that shifted my focus from playing to developing talent and understanding the business of football.

I transitioned into local coaching, dedicating myself to nurturing young South African players. During this time, I founded Thunder United F.C. — my first attempt at creating something bigger. Although the club didn't last long due to my limited business knowledge at the time, it taught me invaluable lessons and ignited a deeper hunger to grow.

Determined to do better, I invested heavily in my education — completing courses in Business Management, Negotiations, and Professional Athlete Management. These weren't just certificates. They were the foundation I needed to operate at the highest level.

Fast forward to today: I have built over 3,000 direct contacts across Europe — straight to club management and coaches, with no third parties or middlemen. This network was strengthened through trusted collaborations with high-profile players including Loris Karius (former Liverpool goalkeeper), Wesley França (Roma), Rayan Vitor (Bournemouth), Vladimir Coufal (former West Ham), and many other top prospects.

Every connection, every late-night call, and every relationship built represents years of persistence, integrity, and a genuine belief in South African talent.

My Vision

To be the most trusted bridge between exceptional South African football talent and the elite clubs and academies of Europe. I don't just move players — I build careers, protect futures, and create pathways that last beyond the pitch.

From the fields of Johannesburg to the floodlights of Europe, the journey continues.
