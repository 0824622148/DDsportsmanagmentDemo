Welcome Cheick Oumar Konaté to the Agency!

We are thrilled to announce the representation of talented Malian right-back Cheick Oumar Konaté! The 22-year-old defender joins our roster as we continue to support and develop the next generation of African football stars on the global stage.

Player Background

Full Name Cheick Oumar Konaté
Date of Birth 2 April 2004 (22 years old)
Nationality Malian
Position Right-Back
Height 1.73m
Preferred Foot Right
Current Club A.E. Kifisia (on loan — Greek Super League)
Parent Club Clermont Foot 63 (Ligue 2, France)
Market Value €1.2M – €1.3M

Born in Bamako, Mali, Cheick began his career in the youth ranks of FC Guidars before earning a professional contract with French side Clermont Foot 63, where he developed his skills in Ligue 2. Known for his strong dribbling, attacking overlaps, and modern full-back qualities, Konaté has already gained valuable experience in European competitions and earned call-ups to the Mali senior national team (Les Aigles).

In 2025/26, he moved on loan to A.E. Kifisia in the Greek Super League, where he has been featuring regularly in their play-out campaign, showcasing his reliability and composure at the back.

With a market value around €1.2M–€1.3M and a reputation as a technically gifted, energetic defender, Cheick brings speed, versatility, and huge potential to any team.

Looking Ahead: A Bright Future

At just 22, Cheick Oumar Konaté is entering a pivotal phase of his career. We see him as a modern full-back — capable of defending solidly while contributing effectively in attack with his dribbling and forward runs.

Our vision for Cheick is clear: continued growth in top-tier European leagues, more senior caps for Mali, and ultimately establishing himself as one of the standout right-backs from the African continent. Whether it's securing a permanent move to a higher-level club or shining on the international stage in upcoming Africa Cup of Nations campaigns, we are fully committed to guiding him toward the highest levels of the game.

Welcome to the family, Cheick! We can't wait to work together and help you achieve your dreams.
