Welcome Derrick Owusu to the Family!

We are thrilled to officially welcome Derrick Owusu to D.D Sports Management Agency! The highly promising 19-year-old Ghanaian central midfielder joins us as we continue to identify and nurture exceptional African talent destined for the global stage.

Who is Derrick Owusu?

Full Name Derrick Owusu
Date of Birth 23 March 2007 (19 years old)
Nationality Ghanaian
Position Central Midfielder / Attacking Midfielder
Height 1.74m
Preferred Foot Left
Current Club Accra Lions (Ghana Premier League)
Academy Charity Stars Football Club

Born on March 23, 2007, Derrick is a versatile, technically gifted midfielder who primarily operates as a central midfielder with the ability to push forward as an attacking midfielder, thanks to his excellent vision, ball control, left-footed precision, and sharp passing range.

Derrick most recently featured for Accra Lions in the Ghana Premier League, where he showcased his composure in midfield and growing influence on games. He developed at Charity Stars Football Club, quickly standing out for his intelligence on the ball, work rate, and natural creativity in the engine room of the team.

At just 19 years old, he already possesses the tactical awareness, technical quality, and physical attributes that modern central midfielders need to thrive at higher levels.

The Road Ahead

Derrick's arrival marks an exciting new chapter for both him and the agency. Our team is fully committed to providing him with top-tier representation, individualized career planning, and the best opportunities to accelerate his development.

Future Plans

  • Securing consistent first-team minutes at a competitive level
  • Strategic exposure to European clubs and international scouting networks
  • Comprehensive professional growth — tactical refinement, physical conditioning, mental preparation, and personal branding
  • A clear long-term pathway toward top European leagues and potential call-ups to the Ghana Black Stars

Derrick brings energy, ambition, creativity, and huge potential to our roster. We're excited to work hand-in-hand with him to unlock his full capabilities and help him reach the highest levels of the game.

A warm welcome to Derrick and his family — we're proud to have you on board! Let's build something special together.
